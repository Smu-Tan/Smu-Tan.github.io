  <p class="about-lead">
    I build and study multilingual language technologies, with a focus on machine translation, translation evaluation, and reasoning-oriented LLMs.
  </p>
</div>

I am a PhD candidate at the <a href="https://ivi.uva.nl/research/language-technology-lab-ltl.html">Language Technology Lab</a>, <a href="https://ivi.uva.nl">University of Amsterdam</a>, working with <a href="https://staff.fnwi.uva.nl/c.monz/">Christof Monz</a> and my colleagues at LTL. Before my PhD, I obtained an M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from <a href="https://www.uu.nl/en">Utrecht University</a>, Netherlands.

My recent work focuses on making machine translation and LLM systems more reliable across languages: learning from human preferences, building reasoning-based evaluation models, studying multilingual transfer, and improving document-level literary translation.
